StartCSS wraps everything in the feature query: @supports (selector(:has(*))) and (color: color-mix(in lab, white, black 50%)) {} to 'cut' older browsers that might break visual presentation. Support for this feature is as follows:

Edge 111+, Chrome 111+, Firefox 121+, Opera 97+, Safari 16.2+, iOS 16.2+, Android 145+ (unable to test earlier than 145)
